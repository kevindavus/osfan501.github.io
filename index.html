My self-summary

I'm a geek, a dad, and husband. I enjoy tinkering with computers and code and I enjoy customization. I thrive in having the freedom to make something truly reflect who I am.

What I’ve worked on

Developing programs for the Navy in partnerships with the FAA and Marines to help keep track of aircraft in flight to ensure they stay safe.

I've also spent a lot of time building side projects on the front end to explore new technologies. Although I'm not currently in a position where I can work on this at my job, I spend as much time outside of work building projects to figure out how different frameworks and tools work.

Side Projects

Home automation software, starting in college for the Solar Decathlon with the Department of Energy and after graduating with my own house. I can't allow myself to have a typical boring house. I want the house to do the brunt of the work whether I'm there or not. I want lights to activate when I walk in or out of a room. I don't want movies to be missed because somebody decided to go to the kitchen for refreshments. I don't want to pay extra on utilities because somebody leaves the TV on at night. I don't want to dig around for my keys, so I have it unlock when I'm recognized.

I'm currently working on a grocery store app that will allow the user to define recipes and encourages units and pictures to be used when adding items to a list to make the shopping experience as simple as possible Ideally it will use location to ping user when there are items in a list and they are near a grocery store. Still in the first steps of this project.
